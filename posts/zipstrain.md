ZipStrain is a tool designed for efficient and scalable strain-level analysis of
metagenomic data. It can profile and compare metagenomics samples at nucleotide
resolution, providing insights into the strain composition of microbial
communities. Check out the GitHub repository for this project:
[ZipStrain](https://github.com/OlmLab/ZipStrain?tab=readme-ov-file).
